Data & Methodology

  • Graph Algorithm: Physics-based spring embedder (COSE) — providers with stronger shared training programs cluster naturally.
  • Node Size: Proportional to √(annual completions) — prevents mega-universities from dominating the canvas.
  • Gap Analysis: Shows CIP training families with <3 providers in the current view. Not an exhaustive census — reflects the top 50 providers by completions only.
  • Inferred Edges represent algorithmic overlap from IPEDS/CIP/SOC mappings, not formal administrative partnerships.
