Grading Methodology

Each occupation is scored from 0 to 100 on six weighted dimensions:

  • 25% — Demand Volume (Annual Openings)
  • 20% — Demand Trajectory (10-year growth %)
  • 20% — Compensation (KC Median Wage)
  • 15% — Local Market Signal (KC QCEW Industry Momentum)
  • 10% — Access/ROI (Wage relative to Training Required)
  • 10% — Future-Proofing (Low Automation Risk)
